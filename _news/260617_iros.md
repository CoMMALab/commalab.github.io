The CoMMA Lab will be presenting seven papers at [IROS 2026](https://2026.ieee-iros.org/)!

["Vectorizing Projection in Manifold-Constrained Motion Planning for Real-Time Whole-Body Control"](/publications#iyer2026mcvamp)
with Shrutheesh Raman Iyer, I-Chia Chang, Andrew Liu, and Yan Gu

["HJCD-IK: GPU-Accelerated Inverse Kinematics through Batched Hybrid Jacobian Coordinate Descent"](/publications#yasutake2026hjcdik)
with Cael Yasutake, Andrew H. Liu, and Brian Plancher

["CDE: Concept-Driven Exploration for Reinforcement Learning"](/publications#mao2026cde)
with Le Mao, Andrew H. Liu, Renos Zabounidis, and Joseph Campbell

["NeHMO: Neural Hamilton-Jacobi Reachability Learning for Decentralized Safe Multi-Arm Motion Planning"](/publications#chen2026nehmo)
with Qingyi Chen and Ahmed H. Qureshi

["Fast Asymptotically Optimal Kinodynamic Planning via Vectorization"](/publications#gao2026pakr)
with Yitian Gao and Andrew Lu

We will also be presenting two RA-L papers:

["Efficient Multi-Robot Motion Planning for Manifold-Constrained Manipulators by Randomized Scheduling and Informed Path Generation"](/publications#guo2026stac)
with Weihang Guo, Kaiyu Hang, and Lydia E. Kavraki

["Variational Shape Inference for Grasp Diffusion on SE(3)"](/publications#bukhari2025graspdiff)
with S. Talha Bukhari, Kaivalya Agrawal, and Aniket Bera
